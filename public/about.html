About

Rights-aware media search for agents and creators.

ZitoAI helps people and software agents find licensable images, sound effects, music tracks, and ambience that fit a real intended use.

What ZitoAI does

ZitoAI translates a natural-language request into a structured media brief, searches the right configured provider, evaluates license metadata, highlights usage restrictions, and creates a License Evidence Pack from genuine source and transaction records.

What ZitoAI does not do

ZitoAI does not issue replacement licenses, transfer rights a provider has made non-transferable, fabricate purchase evidence, guarantee that metadata is correct, or replace advice from a qualified lawyer.

Provider independence

ZitoAI is an independent rights-aware search service. Provider names and trademarks belong to their respective owners. An integration is treated as active only when the relevant credentials, approvals, and API terms permit it.
