About

Rights-aware media procurement.

ZitoAI helps people and software agents find media that fits a real intended use, identify provider-specific restrictions, complete licensing through authorized channels and preserve evidence of the resulting permission.

What Zito does

Zito translates a natural-language request into a structured procurement brief, searches configured catalogs, evaluates license metadata, highlights additional clearances and creates a License Evidence Pack from genuine source and transaction records.

What Zito does not do

Zito does not issue replacement licenses, transfer rights a provider has made non-transferable, fabricate purchase evidence, guarantee that metadata is correct or replace advice from a qualified lawyer.

Provider independence

Zito is an independent procurement tool. Provider names and trademarks belong to their respective owners. An integration is described as connected only after the relevant credentials, approvals and API terms permit it.
